# Introducing Open Ticket AI

We're excited to announce Open Ticket AI, a revolutionary platform for automating ticket classification and management using artificial intelligence.

## What is Open Ticket AI?

Open Ticket AI is an open-source solution that integrates with popular ticketing systems like OTOBO, Znuny, and Zammad to provide intelligent ticket classification, prioritization, and routing.

## Key Features

- **Automated Classification**: Automatically categorize tickets based on content
- **Smart Prioritization**: Intelligently prioritize tickets based on urgency and impact
- **Flexible Integration**: Easy integration with existing ticketing systems
- **Open Source**: Fully open-source and customizable
